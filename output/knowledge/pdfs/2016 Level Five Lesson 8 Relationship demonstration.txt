Recent Situation:
I divorced with my ex-wife in 2011. Since then, I have been together with my girlfriend. At the
end of 2013, I broke up with her. A year later, at the end of 2014, I was reconciled with her. And
our relationship lasts till now.
Here are some of the issues I want to discuss with David:
I’m not quite satisfied with the present relationship. I’m not so sure what kind of partner I need.
I have a fear of marriage, as I reject the bond between two families in the traditional Chinese
marriage. I don’t require my partner to be close to my parents and family, and I’m not willing to
be close to my partner’s parents and family. However, that’s unconventional in Chinese society.
I have a fear of having children. Though there may be various reasons, I cannot find the most
fundamental one.
