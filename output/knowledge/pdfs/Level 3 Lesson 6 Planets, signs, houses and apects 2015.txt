Let’s review what we’ve learned about planets, signs,
houses, Lunar Nodes and aspects!  Remember, astrology
provides an overall outline of a person’s story.
Interpretation allows us to describe, question and
explore the main themes and issues of this story!
 A planet (including the Sun & Moon) is an ACTOR!
 A sign is the ROLE the actor plays.
 A house shows the SETTING/S of the story.
 The Lunar Nodes show the MAIN THEME of the story.
 The aspects show the COMPLICATIONS of the story.
Planets, signs, houses and aspects
have so many possibilities!
EXAMPLE:
18°    37’ 6th House                   18°       47’ 12th House
Think of the different ways this aspect
combination might be interpreted.
1. Perhaps Grandparents/Grandmother played an important
role in their early life because their mother had to work?
2. Such a person might say: “I learned to feel a sense of duty
or obligation to the world when I was growing-up.  “I used to
help my grandmother collect our old clothes and we would
take them to a local community shelter that provided use
clothes for poor families.”
3. Do you think this person would be serious about their
relationships with co-workers?
4.  What might be “negative belief systems” a person with this
combination could have?
Example: No one really sees or appreciates how hard I work to
make our workplace more safe and secure.
