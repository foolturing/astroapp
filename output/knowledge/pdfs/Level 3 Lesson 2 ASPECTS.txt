Did you know that you already use aspects when you talk
about the signs?  The signs are a division of 360°by 12.  Each
sign represents 1/12 of the zodiac or 30 degrees.  Aspects are
also a division of the 360 degree zodiac.
 Everything in our solar system is constantly in motion.
 The most important motions are the orbits of planets around our Sun.
 Every complete orbit of our Sun represents a “cycle.”
 Our Earth’s complete orbit around the Sun equals one year or one yearly cycle.
 Instead of 12 months, in astrology we divide this yearly cycle into 12 “signs.”
 These 12 signs are the calendar of the zodiacand mark a division of our yearly
cycle into 12 signs of 30 degrees each.
12 signs of 30 degrees each = 360 total degrees
Instead of using “days” astrologers measure time by “degrees,” 365.25 days (1 yearly
cycle) = 360 degrees.
These quarter cycles mark our seasons of spring, summer, fall and winter.
The blue arrow on the left points to the
detail of the degrees, in this case you are
looking at a section of the sign Aquarius
pointing to  7 degrees and 36 minutes to
simply show you the sub-division of the 12
signs into 360 degrees.
Here we see the 12 sign division of our
Earth’s yearly orbit around the Sun.
Notice how the 4 cardinal signs divide
this yearly cycle into quarter cycles.

“You know Tian is a Taurus and Mei is the opposite
sign of Scorpio!  They say that opposites sometime
attract, but there can be a lot of tension too!”
All of the popular Sun Sign horoscope literature on compatibility is actually
based upon the aspects of the so called “natural zodiac.”  When you hear
someone saying, “Sagittarius and Virgo are not compatible!” this is because
Virgo and Sagittarius are signs that “square” each other, meaning they are at
a “right angle” to each other on the zodiac wheel.
Think about how these signs are positioned relative to each other, and then
scroll back and look at the diagram that marks all 360 degrees.  In the above
image you can see that Cancer and Capricorn are opposite each other but
that both Capricorn and Cancer are “square” or at a right angle, i.e. 90
degrees, with Libra.  In Sun Sign astrology this would designate Libra as
incompatible with Capricorn and Cancer.  Of course, real life compatibility is
far more complex than this!  However, you can see that already you’ve been
talking about aspects when discussing the compatibility between signs!

Aspect – the angular relationship between two or more planets or points
However, it is important to remember two things:
 Aspects are a matter of degrees – meaning they are measured by degrees
 Aspects are phases or divisions of a cycle – just like the zodiac signs are!
THERE ARE NO GOOD OR BAD ASPECTS!Just like there are no
good or bad signs.  Some aspects are more challenging than others, such
as the square or opposition but the outcome will depend upon how the
person responds to the challenges of their life.  Many people turn
squares into the building blocks of an accomplished life or oppositions
into negotiating skills, and achieve more than those with the so called
easy aspects like trines and sextiles.

In this chart diagram only the Sun, Mercury, Jupiter, Uranus and Neptune are
included in order to easily show how 4 aspects look on a chart wheel.  In this chart
the Sun is square Uranus, Mercury is conjunct Jupiter, while both Mercury and Jupiter
oppose Neptune.
An aspect has what is called an “orb” of influence.  This orb is determined by the
number of degrees the aspect is from being exact.   In the above diagram Mercury is
2⁰ 14’ apart from Jupiter, well within the orb of 7 degrees recommended for a
conjunction.  The Sun is 2⁰ 18’ from an exact square, also well with orb for a square.
How far from exact is Mercury from Neptune?  What about Jupiter and Neptune?

 Houses are WHERE things happen, the setting…
