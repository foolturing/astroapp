Female Student (near age 40)
Career History and Questions
1) Current Occupation:
a) I am a journalist on camera, a director of the news channel at a TV station.
2) Past Occupation:

a) I’ve been professional as the broadcaster, journalist, director of the news programs. I used to
hold a concurrent post of teacher in one college’s institute of Media.
3) Recent confusion at the career development:
a) I have always loved my career in television news. I’ve been an active journalist, director and
broadcaster for many years. I won many prizes related to TV in national, provincial and
municipal contests. However, I seem not to be passionate any more. This may have something
to do with the whole situation of the news industry, and it’s also related to my mental state
and age. I begin to be reluctant to the way of making programs that only quests for fast pace,
live feeling and audience ratings. I want to get the access to my deeper heart. At this time, I
discovered astrology when some critical events happened in my life. I want to know if my birth
chart reveals any sign or revelation related to this. If I want to put more energy and efforts
into astrology, will I be promising on that?
