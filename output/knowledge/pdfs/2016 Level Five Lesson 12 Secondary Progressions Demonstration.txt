On Jun. 5th, 2016, at my 36th birthday, I made a wish about writing. All these years, I always had

myself. In fact, I rarely wrote anything except letters and diaries. I wanted to write novels. I had
many ideas to write. However, when I wanted to write them out, I met many obstacles,
including:
1. The sense of meaninglessness. I felt that everything was meaningless. Nothing could change
this feeling.
2. When I was very young, I felt I was able to know what others were thinking. Therefore, I
thought other people were the same as me. They would understand my inner thoughts when I
