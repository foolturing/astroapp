CAREER?
Astrologers deal with questions and concerns
about career almost as much as questions about
relationships, are there any simple or
reliable techniques that astrologers use in
their career analysis?
Most western astrologers agree that the
“earth houses,” i.e. H2, H6, and H10 form the
basic career triad.  Likewise the planets
associated with these houses become important
career indicators.
Noel Tyl emphasizes the Midheaven or MC, and
the ruler of the MC – along with what he calls
the “reigning need” of the Moon.  Do you agree
with Noel about this?
Yes and no.  The MC is obviously important,
as is the ruler/associate planet of the MC,
and the Moon always represents fundamental
needs.  However, all of these factor MUST be
compared back to main themes of the chart!
Yes, its essential to approach career
analysis holistically!  Without the context
of the whole chart we will miss important
career related concerns.
Give me an example of this.
Suppose someone has Sagittarius Rising, their
need for more personal freedom is certainly
going to influence career and job choices.
Flexibility of hours, belief system, and
travel opportunities impact their decisions.
Okay, but what if this Sagittarius Rising
person has Libra on the MC, and Venus and the
Moon in Cancer in H7?  How does their personal
need for freedom factor into these other very
different needs?
Good question. Libra on the MC with Venus in
Cancer in H7 shows a bit of a theme, i.e.
Libra and H7. They are people oriented like a
consultant.  Depending upon their education
they could become a family therapist.
I see. Their Moon and Venus in Cancer shows a
natural concern with “family” issues, and
Libra and H7 relate to psychology and
counseling. But how does Sagittarius ASC fit?
Being self-employed as a counselor, (even in
a partnership), their work hours depend upon
their income needs.  They also have
flexibility in terms of travel. In addition
they might also teach, and do workshops, etc.
Pay attention to what Jiang Ying is saying.
She is thinking holistically, looking for
themes and considering the whole picture of
the person’s career life astrologically.

Now we see Jupiter conjunct the MC, adding to

Mercury’s birth position in Leo in H8 suggests

Four Keys for Personal Growth Career Analysis
 Career is an evolving
story for everyone.
 Career fulfillment is
actually possible.
 Career issues reflect
early-life patterns.
 People can have more than one career.
This outline allows you to organize your career
analysis of a chart, and more importantly it
helps you to organize the questions you will
ask your client!
I. Basic Career Issues
A. Parental Expectations and Pressures
1. Mother – Moon and Venus
a. nurturing, affection and appreciation
b. tasks and career examples
c. showing how to do things
2. Father – Sun and Mars
a. approval and appreciation
b. showing how to do things
c. tasks and career examples
3. Mother & Father – MC & IC
a. What career related messages were
emphasized by family growing-up?
B. Self-worth
1. 2nd house, ruler of, planets-in, and Venus
a. reward and abundance issues
C. Skill development & education
1. 6th house, ruler of, planets-in, and Mercury
2. 9th house, ruler of, planets-in, and Jupiter
D. Goals
1. 10th house, ruler of, planets-in, and Saturn
a. time and task management questions
b. ability to plan long term
c. handling pressure and responsibility
E. Overriding Life Path Themes
1. Lunar Nodes by Sign and House
2. Planets associated with signs of Nodes
3. Planets associated with houses of Nodes

prepared to talk with your friends or clients
about their career, to help them discover
within themselves the solutions they seek.

Career Fulfillment Sign Test (Yes/No)
ARIES
1. Do you experience “some” immediate gratification in your job each day?
2. Do you have autonomy in how you carry out your tasks?
3. Is your initiative generally rewarded?
4. Are you assigned new projects to direct or carry out?
5. Are your often frustrated by compromises you are forced to accept?
6. Do you experience heated disputes with others every week?
TAURUS
1. Do you handle your tasks smoothly and relatively easily?
2. Do you feel comfortable in your current job?
3. Do you feel adequately compensated financially or otherwise for your efforts?
4. Do others rely upon you and show their appreciation?
5. Do you feel reluctant to come to your job most days?
6. If a stable company offered you 5% more pay would you leave your current job?
GEMINI
1. Are you mentally stimulated by your work?
2. Are your tasks diverse, requiring some multi-tasking?
3. Do you generally experience good communications with your colleagues?
4. Are your ideas listened to and considered in workplace discussions?
5. Are you frequently bored?
6. Do you spend more time texting and surfing with non-work related matters?
CANCER
1. Do you feel secure in your job?
2. Does your financial compensation adequately enable you to create a secure life?
3. Do you feel at home in your job environment?
4. Are your initiatives to improve the security and environment of you workplace generally
successful?
5. Do you often feel insecure in your job?
6. Is your work emotionally gratifying at least half of the time?
LEO
1. Are you proud of the work you do?
2. Is your job fun at least half of the time?
3. Is your authority over your own tasks sufficient enough in your current role?
4. Are you often put in charge of special projects?
5. Are your talents adequately recognized in your current role?
6. Do you feel unrecognized and unappreciated?
VIRGO
1. Are your skills a good fit for your current job?
2. Is your work enabling you to become more of an expert at what you like to do?
3. Do you satisfactorily complete some work related tasks every day?
4. Is your logical or practical approach to problem solving utilized and appreciated?
5. Do you experience nervous anxiety at least half or more of your time at work?
6. Are you frequently sidetracked in your work due to the dysfunction of your workplace and
the lack of clear organization.
LIBRA
1. Do you have good relationships with your colleagues?
2. Are your efforts to create a more harmonious workplace atmosphere appreciated?
3. Are your talents with people being adequately utilized in your current job?
4. Do you some spend time everyday either interacting with customers/clients or providing HR
support?
5. Do others ignore your suggestions or appear to not value your contributions?
6. Do you continue to try and cooperate despite being angry and frustrated at least half the
time?
SCORPIO
1. Does your job provide you with adequate resources to carry out your tasks?
2. Do most of the people you work with seem to trust you?
3. Do you trust at least half of the people you work with?
4. Are you passionate about at least half of the work you do?
5. Do you feel at least 80% secure in your relationships with people in power in your business or
company?
6. Are you envious of at least half of your colleagues?
SAGITTARIUS
1. Do you believe in the work you are doing?
2. Are you enthusiastic about at least half of your career efforts?
3. Does your job offer you some flexibility in hours and lifestyle?
4. Is your job mentally stimulating and conducive to learning new things?
5. Are your ideas and opinions well received at work?
6. Do you often feel trapped by your job?
CAPRICORN
1. Do your efforts earn the respect of others?
2. Are you involved in the planning of tasks?
3. Is there opportunity for you to advance in your current position?
4. Do you accomplish at least half of your goals every week?
5. Are your capabilities being utilized at least 60% in your current role?
6. Do you experience a sense of failure at least half the time in your current job?
AQUARIUS
1. Do you experience a sense of making a contribution in your current job?
2. Are you on friendly terms with at least half of your colleagues?
3. Are your original ideas adequately considered in team meetings?
4. Does your input result in a greater sense of participation?
5. Do you often experience yourself as a loner in your job?
6. Do you fluctuate between indifference and rebelliousness in your attitude towards your job?
PISCES
1. Does your job involve being creative in some way (using your imagination)?
2. Are you inspired by your choice of career at least half the time?
3. Are others (customers/clients) helped by your efforts?
4. Is the atmosphere where you work relaxed and relatively quiet?
5. Does your job often provide you with complex problems to solve?
6. Do you feel uninspired in your job, daydreaming or doing non-work related things?
Career Fulfillment Sign Test ANSWERS
Every answer under Fulfilled = 17 points.  Total fulfillment = 102 points
A score of 85 or above is VERY fulfilled in their current job.
A score of 68 is basically fulfilled in their current job.
A score of 51 is not very fulfilled in their current job.
A score of 34 or lower is quite unfulfilled in the their current job.
