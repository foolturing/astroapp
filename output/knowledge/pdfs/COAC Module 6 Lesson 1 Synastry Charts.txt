Synastry means chart comparison using two natal charts.
The natal chart of one person is put in the center of the chart wheel, while the
natal chart of the other person is put around it in the outer wheel.  Then this
is reversed, with the chart of the person that was in the outer wheel now
placed as the center chart of the wheel with the other person chart around it
in the outer wheel.  In this way a more complete picture of compatibility
issues can be revealed.  The bi-wheel chart is used to make a comparison.
Two Bi-wheel charts allow a complete comparison of both charts to occur.
It is important that both people know their birth times, since the houses are a
significant factor in Synastry.  However, even simple comparison without the
