关系/比较盘 解读示范
太太的星图
我的问题：1、妻子觉得关系更多像是生活合伙人，一起经营家庭很成功，但是觉得亲密度不够、没
有那种心有灵犀的感觉。沟通有问题，无论讨论外部的事务还是说感受、都无法得到丈夫的响应。2、
他特别以事业为重心，对生活缺乏热情，妻子觉得在这方面无法与他同步。
Questions from the wife:
1.
I feel my husband and I are more like life partners, we have been successfully operating
a family, but we don’t have enough intimacy, don’t have soul connection. We have
communication problems. I cannot get response from my husband no matter it’s for
my feelings or outside affairs.
2.
My husband is very career focused, lack passion towards life. I don’t feel I can be on
the same page with him here.
先生的星图
我老公的问题：1、他觉得不能向我敞开心扉，因为他觉得我不能理解他，想法和他不同。2、他认为
妻子就应该把重心放在家庭，以照顾家庭和孩子为主。但是妻子总是和他在事业上平分秋色，在家庭
事务上他也被要求承担，这样他觉得和自己的理念不相符。
Questions from the husband: (they were asked by the wife for the husband.)
1.
My husband doesn’t think he can open up to me, because he doesn’t think I can
understand him, and I have different thoughts from him.
2.
He thinks wife should focus on the family, taking care of the family and child. When the
wife is as good as he is at career, and asks him to share house chores with her, he
doesn’t think that fits his philosophy.
