What astrological symbols or combinations of symbols might be associated
with the following quotes? Briefly explain why:
Example 1.) “I get bored easily… my mind just jumps around all over the place.”
Sample answers: Uranus in the 3rd house.  Mental structure somewhat erratic. Or
possibly Mercury in Gemini “square” Uranus, especially if Mercury were in the 3rd house
and Uranus in the 6th house.  A square denotes conflict, thus a conflict between Mercury
and Uranus could indicate a mind that changes suddenly and shifts focus abruptly.  The
use of the phrase “jumps all over the place” suggests a Uranian influence.
Example 2.)“I need to get away from it all every day, or I start feeling overwhelmed.”
Sample answers:Moon in the 12th house.  The use of the word “need” suggests the
Moon, and 12th house would mean getting “away from it all.”  This would especially be
true of a Moon in the 12th house in Pisces, though it would fit the Moon in the 12th in
any sign.  Or, the Moon conjunct Neptune could also fit.  Emotional sensitivity that
requires solitude and quiet, or an atmosphere that provides a safe haven.
Remember:
THERE ARE A NUMBER OF POSSIBLE ANSWERS THAT MIGHT FIT EACH QUOTE.
