Level Four Lesson 9
Relationship/Synthesis Demonstration 2
Wife’s Chart
妻子问题：我觉得他爱我，以我为主，可以基本上都听我的，配合我对生活的各种安排，但这种
被动性的负面的体现是他不会主动取悦、关心我，比如过生日不会为我精心准备个礼物或者祝福，
任何纪念日都会忘记。让我觉得不被重视和珍惜，很有挫败感，这也是吵架的主要原因。而他不
仅仅对我是被动的，对生活也是被动的，没有任何奋斗和追求，得过且过，爱电影、车、美酒
（不酗酒），但没有为任何事情努力的行动，非常畏难，任何事情都有做不到的理由，每天下班
后就是在看手机小说玩手机游戏中度过，不知道他到底在逃避什么，担忧他的人生发展以及他对
家庭的承担力。
Questions from the wife:
I feel that he loves me, makes me the center, most of the time listens to me, cooperates with life
arrangements I make. But the negative impact of his passiveness is that he would not please me or to
care for me proactively. For example, he would not prepare birthday gift or greetings for me, he would
forget every anniversary. He’s also passive in his life, has no ambition or goals. He’s just getting by. He
loves movie, car, fine wine (not alcoholic), hasn’t made effort for anything. Always has excuses for not to
do hard work. Everyday after work, he would just read novels or play games on his phone. I don’t know
what he’s escaping from. I am worried about his life development direction and his ability to be
responsible for the family.
Husband’s Chart
丈夫问题： 跟岳父岳母生活习惯和价值观几乎全面冲突，但是平日往来密切，躲都躲不过。爱人
自幼受岳父训斥教育的熏陶，被我触犯以后每每以上司对下属，家长对子女的语气强烈训斥我，
而且每次话题都无限扩张无限延伸，且常伴有用词过当的情况，令我不堪忍受。
Questions from the husband:
I have almost entirely different life habit and philosophy from my parents in law. But we have close
interaction in daily life, I cannot avoid them. My wife has been deeply influenced by my father in law’s
scolding philosophy since she’s young. Whenever I do something wrong, my wife would scold me as a
boss to her employ, or a parent to the child. She would be very harsh and mean when she scolds me,
and could go very far with the topic. I cannot bear that.
