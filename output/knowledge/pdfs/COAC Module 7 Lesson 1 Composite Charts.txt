Mercury and Venus are NEVER very far from the Sun, they can NEVER oppose the Sun from a
geocentric perspective.  To keep Mercury and or Venus in line with this“rule,” always make sure
that the Sun is calculated correctly first.  Then make sure that neither Mercury nor Venus is on the
opposite side of the chart from the Sun.                                                                                           1
