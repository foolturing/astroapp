Take a look again at the charts of Brian Wilson and Paul McCartney,
both of them have the Sun square Neptune.  Is this square waxing
or waning?
ANSWER: The square between the Sun and Neptune is waning.
Okay, so this means that both of them shared a waning or Capricorn
kind of square.  So in their case this makes the square more like
Capricorn, more competitive and more creatively ambitious, right?
Right!  If this has been a waxing square their might still have been
a compelling urge to be creative, but this would’ve had a cancer
quality to it, and would’ve been more about their internal
security needs.  In Paul’s case, sense he has the Sun in the 10th
house already, this additional Capricorn symbolism reinforces his
ambition and need to succeed.
So, maybe Paul would’ve suffered more from “stage fright” if he
